NordLabs Privacy Notice

Introduction

The Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of Nord's websites, applications, and Services which also apply to the NordLabs product. However, providing Services related to NordLabs also involves processing additional personal data.

Additional Personal Data Processed When Providing NordLabs Services

In addition to the information provided in the Privacy Policy, we process the following data when you use NordLabs Services:

Information Collected on NordLabs Website

We collect your email address to inform you about access to NordLabs products. Registration requires only your email address, which can be opted out at any time by emailing us at labs@nordvpn.com.

AI Phishing Assistant

AI Phishing Assistant is an anti-phishing extension created by Nord using assets provided by OpenAI. It scans users' emails to detect phishing attempts.

To log in, you need a NordAccount with the same email address used in NordLabs registration. We process your email for authentication and feedback purposes only. No personal data from your emails is stored. Your email data will be retained by OpenAI for a maximum of 30 days before deletion.

Link Checker

Link Checker is a tool to identify malicious URLs. No personal data is processed, only scanned URLs and analysis results are retained for analytical purposes. Feedback is collected anonymously.

File Checker

File Checker is a tool to scan and identify malicious files. No personal data is processed, only the file hash and analysis results are retained for analytical purposes. Feedback is collected anonymously.
